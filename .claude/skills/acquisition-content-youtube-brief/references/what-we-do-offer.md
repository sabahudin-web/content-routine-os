# What Sabahudin Does & Offers

**Last updated:** 2026-03-15

---

## Primary Role

**AI Social Selling Partner** — helps consultants and small business owners implement AI-powered social selling systems on LinkedIn.

He is NOT an agency. He is a solopreneur who works hands-on with clients to build and implement systems — not to manage them long-term.

---

## Core Service: Claude Code Setup

Saba's primary offer is setting up a complete Claude Code environment for clients — AI agents, skills, workflows, and MCP integrations that automate the operational side of social selling:

- LinkedIn list building (ICP-targeted)
- Outreach and DM workflows via Aimfox
- Prospect enrichment via BrightData
- CRM integration via HubSpot
- Content creation workflows (newsletter, LinkedIn posts, YouTube)

**Who it's for:** Consultants and SMB owners who want AI working for them on LinkedIn, but can't build it themselves.

---

## Secondary Project: Unstuck Community

A community for people stuck in their business or career. Still being built.

**Who it's for:** Solopreneurs and professionals who feel stuck — not making progress, not sure what to do next.
