# Sabahudin Murtic — Profile & Background

**Last updated:** 2026-03-15

---

## Who He Is

Sabahudin "Sab" Murtic is an AI Social Selling Partner who helps consultants and small business owners implement AI-powered social selling systems on LinkedIn. He operates as a solopreneur with a VA and a design support person.

He is also building the **Unstuck Community** — a community for people stuck in their business or career.

---

## Origin Story (Use This for Storytelling Angles)

- **Born 1993 in Bosnia** — during the war in Yugoslavia
- **Father died during the war** — raised by his mother and sister alone
- Grew up in a poor environment in the early 2000s; moved frequently because they had no home
- Most of childhood spent on the streets — "the streets taught me many things school couldn't"
- Before high school: mostly introverted, trying to find his place
- At 15: earned first money drawing architectural projects for classmates and selling them
- University in 3 years — but realized he knew almost nothing about the real world

**The long detour:**
- Spent years talking about starting something with friends but rarely acting
- "For almost ten years I kept telling my friends: We should start this. We should build that. But I rarely took action."
- Worked in call centers and tourism — learned how to work with people
- "My biggest mistake was talking more than doing."

**The turning point:**
- Was working a normal 9–5, applying for jobs daily — most ended in rejection
- Started posting on LinkedIn — not to build a business, but to improve English and document learning
- People started reaching out. First freelance projects came in.
- Launched digital product "50+ GPTs" — sold quickly
- Signed first two high-ticket clients → quit his job with no backup plan, ~€50k of debt
- Mentor Jasmin Alić entered the picture — "Today he is not only a friend but also a mentor"

**What he built:**
- Helped 30+ founders and consultants grow on LinkedIn
- Built 100+ custom AI workflows
- Helped clients go from invisible to fully booked
- Now runs AI Social Selling business + expanding to YouTube

**Now:**
- Hired first childhood friend — "the dream is slowly becoming reality"
- Still figuring it out in public — shares the messy path, not the polished version
