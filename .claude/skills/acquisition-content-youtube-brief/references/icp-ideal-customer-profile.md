# ICP — Ideal Customer Profile for youahudin's YouTube Channel

**Last updated:** 2026-03-15

---

## Who Watches (and Who Should Watch)

Saba's YouTube channel serves the same audience he works with: **consultants and small business owners who want to grow their business through AI-powered social selling on LinkedIn** — but YouTube also reaches solopreneurs earlier in their journey who aren't ready to hire yet.

---

## Three Audience Segments

### Segment 1: Independent Consultants
- **Who:** Solo practitioners, fractional executives, independent advisors (like Michel Chapuis)
- **Situation:** Have established expertise and a few clients, but don't have a reliable system for generating new business
- **Goal:** Build a repeatable pipeline without spending on ads or relying on referrals alone
- **Pain:** Inconsistent deal flow; uncomfortable with self-promotion; don't know how to use AI or LinkedIn systematically
- **They respond to:** Specific systems, real examples, proof that non-techie consultants can implement this

### Segment 2: SMB Owners
- **Who:** Owners of small service businesses (agencies, training firms, boutique firms)
- **Situation:** Currently rely on referrals, word of mouth, or paid ads that don't convert
- **Goal:** Generate leads directly from LinkedIn without a big team or big budget
- **Pain:** Tried LinkedIn but got no results; confused by tools; overwhelmed by AI hype
- **They respond to:** Step-by-step walkthroughs, before/after comparisons, realistic time investments

### Segment 3: Solopreneurs (Earlier Stage)
- **Who:** One-person businesses, freelancers pivoting to consulting, people building their first offer
- **Situation:** Good at what they do but struggling to attract clients consistently
- **Goal:** Land first clients or scale from inconsistent to $5–10K/month
- **Pain:** Chasing clients manually, spending too much time on outreach with low conversion, don't know where to start with AI
- **They respond to:** Raw honesty about what works, what Saba actually does, mistakes he made

---

## Unified Profile

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Role | Consultant, SMB owner, or solopreneur in a service business |
| Experience | Has real expertise; not a beginner in their field |
| LinkedIn presence | Exists but inconsistent or underperforming |
| AI familiarity | Curious about AI but not deeply technical; has tried ChatGPT |
| Goal | More clients through LinkedIn without more manual effort |
| Fear | Wasting time on systems that don't work; looking unprofessional |
| Core desire | A system they can implement themselves, that actually produces results |

---

## Who They Are NOT

- Complete beginners to business (no offer, no skills yet)
- Senior executives at large corporations (they have BD teams)
- Tech founders building SaaS products (different audience entirely)
- People who want theory — they want implementation
- Vanity metric chasers — they care about clients, not follower counts

---

## What Makes Them Click (YouTube Specifically)

- **Specific numbers and real results:** "370 leads from 5,000 connections" beats "grow your LinkedIn"
- **Saba's raw honesty:** They trust him because he admits mistakes openly
- **"Screenshot and implement today":** Pillar 1 content must be immediately actionable
- **No fog:** They're tired of polished LinkedIn gurus selling vague frameworks
- **Relatable struggle:** They've been where Saba was — stuck, invisible, grinding without results
