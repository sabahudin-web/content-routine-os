# Newsletter Subject Line Generator

Generate 5-10 subject lines using 100 battle-tested frameworks matched to edition intent.

## Process

### Step 1: Analyze Edition Intent
Identify: primary goal, core emotion, key hook elements.

### Step 2: Match 3-5 Framework Categories

| Edition Type | Best Categories |
|---|---|
| Educational | Curiosity, Quick Win, Contrarian |
| Story-based | Story, Pain Point, Transformation |
| Authority | Authority, Social Proof, Results |
| Roundup | Curiosity, Value, Quick Win, Trend |
| Launch | Action, Empowerment, Urgency |

### Step 3: Generate 5-10 Lines
Each: subject line + framework name + preview text.

## Style Rules

- Under 50 chars when possible
- Specific numbers > vague claims
- Direct, no-BS voice
- Lowercase often wins
- Questions > statements for cold audiences
- **Avoid:** Unlock, Discover, Revolutionary, Game-changing, Don't miss

## 100 Frameworks (by Trigger)

**CURIOSITY (9):** Is-It-Worth-It, Pattern Interrupt, Surprising Success, Celebrity Twist, Hidden Knowledge, Weird Question, Earnings Curiosity, Inside Look, High-Stakes Question

**URGENCY (5):** FOMO Warning, Time-Sensitive, Obsolete Method, High-Stakes Decision, Year-Start Prep

**PAIN POINT (11):** Rock Bottom, Pain Reveal, Relatable Struggle, Invisible Mistake, Hidden Mistake, Unspoken Reason, Costly Mistake, Why Everyone Struggles, Common Trap, Wishful Thinking, Failure Stat

**AUTHORITY (6):** Name Drop, Insider Giveaway, Niche Authority, Authority Challenge, Niche Poll, Niche Audit

**RESULTS (11):** Short-Term Win, Instant Results, Outlier Success, Growth Snapshot, Top Performer, ROI Revelation, First Milestone, Exact Path, High ROI, 10x Multiplier, Accidental Success

**CONTRARIAN (11):** Positive Reframe, I Was Wrong, Dark Side, Hidden Cost, Unexpected Truth, Bad Advice, Success Despite, Not-What-You-Think, What-If, Don't Do This, Paradoxical

**VALUE (11):** Compressed Value, Freebie Teaser, Different Roads, Perfect Plan, Irresistible Free, Niche Playbook, Anatomy Hook, Annual Playbook, Template, Hidden Gem, Timeless Secrets

**ACTION (10):** Do-This-Now, Skip Downside, Stop/Start, Instant Upgrade, Turn X Into Y, Hate-to-Love, Do-Or-Die, Exit Strategy, Old Way, Yes-You-Can

**EMPOWERMENT (6):** Yes You Can, Less-Is-More, One Thing, Challenge Acceptance, Targeted Callout, Implied Interest

**STORY (6):** Miraculous Insight, High-Stakes Failure, Trial & Error, Love-Hate, Big Loss, Unsuccessful Approach

**TREND (4):** Tech Shift, Breakthrough, Weird Works, Unconventional

**QUICK WIN (10):** Little-Known Trick, Fast Win, Equation, Quick Challenge, Mini Rant, Overlooked Factor, Facts Over Hype, Damn Good, Insert Here, Cliche Disruptor
