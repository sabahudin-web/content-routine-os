## How To Use This File

Each story below follows this structure:
- **Title** — the name you give this story
- **The situation** — what was happening, who was involved, when
- **The moment** — the turning point, the insight, the failure, the win
- **The lesson** — what you took away from it
- **Emotional core** — what feeling does this story carry? (embarrassment, pride, fear, clarity, etc.)
- **Themes** — what topics does this story connect to? (can be used for multiple content pillars)
